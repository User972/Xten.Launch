— Frequently asked questions

Honest answers, no spin.

Not answered? WhatsApp our team — usually answered within 30 minutes during business hours.

How long does it take to reach band 7?

It depends on your starting level. From band 5.5, usually 8–12 intensive weeks. We give a firm estimate after the free diagnostic test — including which skill to strengthen first.

Is there a band-score guarantee?

We don't sell empty promises. But >90% of our students reach their target band on the first or second attempt. If you don't, we give free extra classes — not an unrealistic refund.

Are the teachers native speakers?

Some are. We also have Indonesian teachers certified in CELTA / TESOL — many students prefer to start with a local teacher and move to a native one once their speaking is fluent.

What if I can't attend a class?

Every class is recorded — you can re-watch anytime in the student portal. We also schedule make-up classes with a group at your level.

Can you help book the official IELTS exam?

Yes — we partner with British Council and IDP. We help you choose the centre, date, and exam type (Computer-based or Paper-based) to fit your timeline.

Are there online classes?

Yes, we run hybrid classes — choose offline at our centre or live online via Zoom. Many students use both interchangeably.

What's the difference between IELTS Academic and General Training?

Academic is for university; General is for work, immigration, or training. We help you pick the right one in your consultation session.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes — we accept 0% installments with several banks (BCA, Mandiri, BRI), plus QRIS and virtual account payments.
